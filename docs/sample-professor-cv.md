## Research summary

The Cognitive Dynamics Lab studies how attentional networks in humans adapt under sleep deprivation and time pressure, combining behavioral experiments, wearable sensing, and large-scale fMRI. We build statistical models in R and Python and collaborate with machine learning and clinical partners; work has implications for preclinical and translational work in circadian and mood-related disorders. The team welcomes trainees who are comfortable in both the wet-lab (behavioral/physiology) and computational space.
